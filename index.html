Folded

Letters to your future self

Folded is an iOS app for writing letters to your future self. Seal a letter, choose a delivery date, and read it again when the date arrives.

Sealed is sealed. The date is the commitment. The reading happens when you're ready.

Support

Questions, feedback, or trouble with the app? Send a note to foldedlettersapp@gmail.com and we'll get back to you.

Privacy

Folded stores nothing in the cloud, your letters live only on your device. Read the full privacy policy.
